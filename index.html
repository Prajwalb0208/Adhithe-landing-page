About Adhithe

AI-Powered Learning, Textbook-Grounded

Combining the reliability of official textbooks with the power of artificial intelligence

Adhithe is a revolutionary AI-powered learning platform designed for Indian students from classes 5 to 12. It uses only official NCERT and state-board textbooks as its reference sources, ensuring accuracy, reliability, and perfect alignment with the school curriculum.

Our advanced AI system retrieves exact textbook content and intelligently converts it into clear articles, short audio lessons, concise videos, interactive sandboxes, or runnable code demonstrations. Everything is available in both English and major Indian languages.

By staying fully rooted in official educational material and enhanced with AI capabilities, Adhithe provides a trustworthy, intelligent way to learn, revise, and understand subjects without relying on uncertain online information.
